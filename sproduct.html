Product Details

Timex Essential TW2U05500 is an amazing and interesting Ladies watch from Originals Update collection. Case is made out of Stainless Steel while the dial colour is Rose. The features of the watch include (among others) a chronograph. 30 metres water resistancy will protect the watch and allows it to be worn in scenarios where it is likely to be splashed but not immersed in water. It can be worn while washing your hands and will be fine in rain. We ship it with an original box and a guarantee from the manufacturer.
